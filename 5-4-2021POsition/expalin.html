STATIC Relative Absolute Fixed Sticky 
    How to position ? 

  1. Determine the method of positioning

        position:static;
        position:relative;
        position:absolute;
        position:fixed;
        position:sticky;

 2. Specify the actual numerical values for
        its exact coordinates

        left:100px;
        top:50px;
        right:200px;
        bottom:5px;

position-property- STATIC

    Default setting
  • Positions itself according to the “normal flow” (order of declaration of elements in the HTML, etc)
  • rules like left:10px; work top:30%; do not work
I'm the other sibling element.
All eyes on me. I am the main element.
I'm the other sibling element.

position-property- Relative

    Positions itself according to its default position
  • Still follows the “normal flow”, maintains its previous space occupied
  • position:relative; top:50px; left:50px; box moves 50px down (away from the top edge), and 50px to the right (away from the left edge) from its previous position
I'm the other sibling element.
All eyes on me. I am the main element.
I'm the other sibling element.

position-property- Absolute

  • Element is removed/lifted from the container element, and positioned according to the edges of the canvas
  • position:absolute; top:0px; left:0px; box is attached to the upper-left corner of the canvas, not the container element
I'm the other sibling element.
All eyes on me. I am the main element.
I'm the other sibling element.

Fixed

Fixed position elements are similar to absolutely positioned elements. They are also removed from the normal flow of the document.
